Zeroth

Advancing Tomorrow's Defense & Today's Innovation


In an era where national security is defined by technological superiority, Zeroth exists to ensure India is never left behind.


We are a defense intelligence and sourcing firm that identifies, vets, and delivers frontier technologies from the world’s most advanced innovation ecosystems — including the United States, Israel, Europe, and Russia — directly into the hands of Indian defense institutions.


Our work is not transactional. It is transformational.



What We Do



Strategic Technology Scouting


We identify mission-critical innovations — from autonomous platforms and cyber systems to advanced sensing and AI — long before they reach conventional markets.


Buy vs Build Advisory


We serve as a trusted advisor to many establishments, and defense OEMs — delivering high-fidelity analysis on when to acquire, when to build, and when to co-develop technology


Export Control & Policy Navigation


We understand the geopolitical, legal, and regulatory complexity of international defense sourcing — and manage it with precision


Supply chain Architecture

From licensing and localization to deployment and lifecycle support — we engineer the entire pipeline from foreign lab to Indian frontline.
